Oranges are a type of citrus fruit that are known for their sweet and tangy flavor. They are a rich source of vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants. Oranges can be eaten fresh, juiced, or used in a variety of recipes, including salads, desserts, and sauces. The fruit is also used to produce orange oil, which is used in cleaning products, perfumes, and aromatherapy. Oranges are typically grown in warm climates and are harvested in the winter months. They come in several varieties, including navel, Valencia, and blood oranges, each with its own unique flavor and characteristics.
